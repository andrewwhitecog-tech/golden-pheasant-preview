Our Story

A small painting company built around careful work

Golden Pheasant Painting began with a simple belief: a finish only looks effortless when the preparation underneath it is disciplined. The bird represents patience, precision, and a little brightness brought into everyday spaces.

This is an owner-operated service. The person who learns the project, prepares the scope, and answers the questions stays accountable through the final walkthrough. We keep the process legible, the work area orderly, and the promises specific.

PreparationClean, repair, mask, sand, and prime before finish coats begin.
CraftCrisp lines, even coverage, and the right coating for the surface.
StewardshipProtect the home, communicate clearly, and leave the space in good order.
AccountabilityWalk the finished work together and resolve details before closeout.
Why Golden Pheasant

The golden pheasant doesn't rush its plumage

  • Owner on every job. The person who quoted your project is the person holding the brush.
  • Prep-first philosophy. We'd rather lose a bid than skip the sanding.
  • Respect for your home. Clean drop cloths, daily tidy-up, shoes off where it counts.
  • Straight answers. Real timelines, real prices, and a phone that gets answered.

Our promise

Every bird earns its feathers one at a time — and every home earns its finish one careful coat at a time. If a line isn't crisp or a wall isn't covered, we make it right before we ask to be paid.
