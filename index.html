Services

Everything a house needs to look its best

From a single accent wall to a full exterior repaint, we treat every job like it's our own home — because word of mouth is our whole business.

🛋️

Interior Painting

Walls, ceilings, trim, doors, and accent work. Furniture protected, edges razor-sharp, rooms back in service fast.

🏡

Exterior Painting

Pressure-wash, scrape, sand, prime, and two finish coats built for wet winters and summer sun. Siding, trim, fascia, and doors.

🚪

Cabinets & Trim

Factory-smooth sprayed finishes on kitchen cabinets, built-ins, and millwork — the highest-impact upgrade per dollar in the house.

🪵

Decks & Fences

Stains and sealers that bring tired wood back to life and protect it for years of barbecues to come.

🩹

Drywall & Repair

Patches, texture matching, caulking, and peeling-paint rescue — the prep work that separates a paint job from a finish.

🎨

Color Consultation

Not sure which white is the white? We'll sample, compare in your light, and help you land colors you'll love for a decade.

Our Process

No surprises, start to finish

Walk & Talk

We walk the job with you, listen to what you want, and take real measurements — not guesses.

Clear Written Quote

One page, plain English: scope, prep, products, coats, timeline, and price. It doesn't change unless you change the job.

Prep Like It Matters

Masking, sanding, priming, repairs. Ninety percent of a great finish happens before the finish coat.

Paint & Perfect

Quality coatings applied right, a final walkthrough with you, and we don't leave until you're happy.
